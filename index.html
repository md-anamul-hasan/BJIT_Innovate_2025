The 3-Week Journey

The competition is a fast-paced sprint. Click on each week to see the detailed activities and required deliverables for that phase.

Ideation & Foundation

  • Activity: Kickoff & Launch Event, Team Formation.
  • Activity: Problem Identification & "Mentor Office Hours".
  • Knockout Phase: 5-minute concept pitch to judges.
  • Deliverable: 2-page concept brief and presentation slide.

Design & Development

  • Activity: Detailed CAD Design in SolidWorks & Simulation (FEA/CFD).
  • Activity: Prototyping (3D Printing & Embedded Systems).
  • Activity: Optional Workshop on Embedded Systems.
  • Deliverable: Midway progress report, initial CAD models, and simulation results.

Refinement & Finals

  • Activity: Prototype testing, refinement, and final adjustments.
  • Activity: Final presentation and documentation preparation.
  • Grand Finale: Live demonstration to judges and stakeholders.
  • Deliverable: Fully functional prototype, final design documents, and presentation.
